The Author

Originally from the high plains of Colorado, the author now lives on the coastal bend of Texas with her husband and three rescue cats. She was an educator for 26 years where she taught 2nd grade and Special Education. In retirement, she has taught both Reading and ESL to adults. With a green thumb and landscaper's eye, she enjoys the labors of gardening as a way of life.

The Illustrator

Scottie Anderson was born and raised in Durand, Wisconsin. Her early passion for art developed into an art degree from the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire. Now retired, Scottie continues her art career doing personalized paintings for clients.
