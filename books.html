This is a true story of a very curious rescue cat named Sally Mae. She liked to ride on anything that moved. She liked slow moving things, and she liked fast moving things. Always curious, she would seek out those things that would give her a ride. Curiosity is a good thing. Through curiosity Sally Mae learned new things about her world every day. Most importantly, Sally Mae learned that while it is good to be curious, it is also good to be cautious because adventure may lead a curious cat into danger.

"Curious Adventure was a most delightful book, proving without a shadow of doubt that our feline friends share our desire for adventure and at the same time cherish a safe and loving home. "
